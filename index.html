How it works

From factory floor to EU registry
in under 20 minutes

01 / COLLECT
W

Send via WhatsApp

Supervisor photographs packing lists, GOTS certificates, and test reports. Sends to your factory's dedicated VeriPass WhatsApp number. No app, no login, no training.

⏱ 2 minutes · Tamil + English
02 / EXTRACT
AI

AI reads documents

Claude AI extracts fiber composition, supplier names, certification details, and HS codes. Flags missing mandatory fields. Sends confirmation summary back via WhatsApp.

⏱ 90 seconds · 15 ESPR fields
03 / VERIFY
✓

Compliance checked

System checks ESPR mandatory fields, REACH substance restrictions, and certificate expiry dates. Human review queue for edge cases. Pass rate tracked per exporter.

⏱ 2 minutes · ESPR + REACH
04 / REGISTER
⬡

DPP anchored + filed

JSON-LD DPP generated, anchored on Hyperledger Besu blockchain, and registered with EU CIRPASS. QR code and PDF label downloaded. Buyer notified instantly.

⏱ 90 seconds · GS1 QR ready
